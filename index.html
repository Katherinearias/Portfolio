Hi, Im Katherine. I am a UX/UI designer. Born in Caracas, Venezuela, raised in Atlanta, Ga. I am observant, calm and dedicated . I believe design should be purposeful and invite users in, while keeping it relevant to its original intent.


More About Me


I learned UX/UI design through a Georgia Tech Bootcamp in Atlanta, Ga. I have always loved design and even started a streetwear clothing company in high school. I have a background in customer service in the food industry. My organization and focus has resulted in moving up in multiple work experiences. I like being passionate about what I’m doing and I think that shows in my results.


I have worked on projects focused on user research, wireframing & UI design. When creating designs, I constantly refer back to my user research to make sure the intent of my design is in tact.


Outside of UX, there’s a few things I enjoy. This includes coffee, cooking, music, traveling, conventions & museums. If I’m traveling I definitely try to find a good coffee shop in the area. I’m a big fan of going to live shows and try to attend any show a band/musician I like is having in my area. I think my interests have a big impact on how to spark my creativity.
